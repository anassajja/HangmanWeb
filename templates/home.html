ABOUT US

This is a version of the classic letter guessing game called Hangman. You are shown a set of empty letters that match a word

or phrase and you must guess what those letters are to reveal the hidden word. You guess by drawing letters from those

displayed on the sides. If you choose a letter that is in the word, a sound is played and that letter is revealed among the

empty letters. I hope you will have fun playing this game and that you can learn or improve your French at the same time.

This game was made by the RRRA group. All comments or suggestions are welcome. To contact us. anass.ajja@ynov.com
